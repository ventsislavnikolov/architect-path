# Resources

High-trust resources grounding the lessons. Tier: ⭐ = primary source (read/watch first).

## Architect role & operating model
- ⭐ **The Software Architect Elevator** — Gregor Hohpe (O'Reilly). The architect as the
  person who rides between board room and engine room; make others smarter; decision
  discipline over buzzwords. https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/the-software-architect/9781492077534/
  - Author hub: https://architectelevator.com/
  - Summary: https://www.jonas.rs/2024/10/03/book-summary-the-software-architect-elevator.html
- **The Architect Elevator (free essays)** — https://architectelevator.com/

## Architecture fundamentals & trade-offs
- ⭐ **Fundamentals of Software Architecture** — Mark Richards & Neal Ford (O'Reilly).
  First Law: *everything is a trade-off*; aim for the "least worst" architecture; deriving
  architecture characteristics from business needs. https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/fundamentals-of-software/9781492043447/
  - Notes: https://bagerbach.com/books/fundamentals-of-software-architecture/
  - Notes: https://danlebrero.com/2021/11/17/fundamentals-of-software-architecture-summary/
- **Software Architecture: The Hard Parts** — Ford, Richards, Sadalage, Dehghani.
  Modern trade-off analysis for distributed systems. https://www.amazon.com/Software-Architecture-Trade-Off-Distributed-Architectures/dp/1492086894

## Design canon (judgment)
- ⭐ **A Philosophy of Software Design** — John Ousterhout. Complexity is the enemy; deep vs shallow
  modules; information hiding; change amplification & cognitive load.
  https://blog.pragmaticengineer.com/a-philosophy-of-software-design-review/ ·
  notes: https://bagerbach.com/books/a-philosophy-of-software-design/
- ⭐ **The Pragmatic Programmer** — Hunt & Thomas. ETC, DRY, orthogonality, tracer bullets,
  broken windows. Tips: https://pragprog.com/tips/ ·
  summary: https://github.com/HugoMatilla/The-Pragmatic-Programmer

## Documenting & governing decisions
- ⭐ **ADRs — the home page & ecosystem** — https://adr.github.io/
- **ADR examples & templates (Joel Parker Henderson)** — https://github.com/architecture-decision-record/architecture-decision-record
- **ADRs + C4 integration** — https://medium.com/decathlondigital/software-architecture-architecture-decision-record-c4-11ceff211baf
- **C4 model** — https://c4model.com/ (referenced via search; canonical site)
- **Fitness functions** — a decision record *documents*, a fitness function *assures*;
  runs in CI to catch architectural drift. (Building Evolutionary Architecture — Ford/Parsons/Kua)
